Right, but the problem is that Sandy ended on November 2nd. The specific criterion for whether you are counted as having a job in a given month is whether you received a paycheck for a pay period including the 12th of that month. So if a restaurant that pays weekly closed for the week of the 12th, which is exactly when Irma hit, all of its workers would be counted as not having jobs. Same for any gas station or hotel or anything else that has primarily hourly workers.
