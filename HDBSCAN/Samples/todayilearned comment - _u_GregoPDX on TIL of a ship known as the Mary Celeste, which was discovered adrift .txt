/u/GregoPDX on TIL of a ship known as the Mary Celeste, which was discovered adrift and deserted in the Atlantic. The last log entry was 10 days before her discovery and when another ship happened upon her, all of the provisions and belongings of the crew were perfectly intact, but the crew were all missing
Kramer talks about this in his book 'Astonishing Tales of the Sea'. He also covers ship sinkings like the Andrea Doria, which collided with the Stockholm in dense fog 12 miles off the coast of Nantucket.
