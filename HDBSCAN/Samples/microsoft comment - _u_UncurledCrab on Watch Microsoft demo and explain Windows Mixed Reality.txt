MR is the term that describes the platform, which encompass a continuum of VR to AR. The headsets coming this month are on the VR end of the spectrum, but they are called MR headsets because of their compatibility with the platform and its ability to combine your surroundings with programs and new environments. The video in the article does a great job demonstrating the whole MR vision.
