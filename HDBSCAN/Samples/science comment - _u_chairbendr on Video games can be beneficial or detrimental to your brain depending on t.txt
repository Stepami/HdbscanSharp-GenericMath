/u/chairbendr on Video games can be beneficial or detrimental to your brain depending on the navigation strategy and the genre of the game, neuroscientists show in new study
Games in the same genre can present very unique cognitive challenges. Game genres are not even universally agreed-upon. Some might consider 3d platformers to be action games. Some action games might involve 3d platforming. Even IF there was relatively strong agreement on genre, we would still have the problem that every game, regardless of genre, can present unique cognitive challenges between which our learned skills do not generalize. The devil is in the details.
